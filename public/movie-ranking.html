Best Movies according to Puja

My top 3 movies of all time

Whisper Of The Heart

It's a light-hearted, slice-of-life movie. I love the where the female lead works hard in trying to write a book to know if she is a good writer or if she needs to study more. Like working hard for something to know yourself better.

Inside Out

The movie shows that bad times are what makes the happy times really significant.

Dear Zindagi

A beautiful and introspective film.
